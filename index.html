Our Story

Fashion rooted
in craft & conscience.

Drape was founded on a single conviction — that Indian fashion doesn't need to apologise for being Indian. Our pieces draw from centuries of textile tradition and bring them into the present with quiet confidence.

Every fabric is sourced from Indian weavers. Every print is hand-blocked by artisans. Every silhouette is designed to move beautifully on the Indian woman's body.

"We make clothes for women who know exactly who they are — and dress accordingly."
